About Me

My name is Emma Hardison and I am a student studying computer science at University of Colorado, Boulder. I will be graduating this May! My journey was a little unconventional, having come from an arts and social science background, my entrance into STEM and computer science took a lot of hard work and discipline. My love for math and computer science only grows as I learn more, and I am grateful that in this ever changing industry there will never be a lack of new skills to work on! I am currently sharpening my skills in Kubernetes and Golang as a software developer intern at NetApp. Additionally, I am learning Angular for my senior capstone project. My favorite part of work is when I get chances to problem solve, so I am looking for a career that always has interesting challenges to tackle. I also feel fulfilled when my coworkers and I can have fun helping each other debug, so I look forward to being part of a friendly and passionate work environment at my next developer job. Though I have enjoyed my time immensely as an intern, I am looking for a full-time junior software engineer position for after I graduate.
Outside of academics and the workplace, I enjoy drawing, crochet, and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. I believe both of these hobbies help keep me sharp and creative, and help my assets as an engineer improve. They also keep me healthy and happy!
